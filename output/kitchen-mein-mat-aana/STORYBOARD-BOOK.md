# STORYBOARD BOOK

## Kitchen Mein Mat Aana

**Format:** YouTube Short | 9:16 Vertical | 1080x1920
**Duration:** ~60 seconds (8 scenes x ~7.5s each)
**Language:** Hinglish (Roman script)
**Tone:** Cinematic family drama — betrayal to redemption

---

## CHARACTER BIBLE

### MEERA (Protagonist)
- **Age:** 23
- **Face:** Warm brown skin, large expressive dark brown eyes, long thick black hair usually in a braid, gentle oval face, dimpled smile when happy
- **Early Outfit (Scenes 1-5):** Simple light green cotton salwar kameez, silver jhumka earrings, glass bangles, dupatta draped over shoulder, no makeup
- **Later Outfit (Scenes 6-8):** Elegant white chef coat with gold embroidery, hair tied in a professional bun with a fresh flower, minimal gold jewelry, confident posture
- **Arc:** Hopeful bride -> humiliated daughter-in-law -> secret hustler -> celebrity chef -> compassionate victor

### SAVITRI (Antagonist / Saas)
- **Age:** 55
- **Face:** Sharp features, thin pursed lips, heavy-lidded judgmental eyes, grey-streaked hair pulled into a tight bun, imposing posture
- **Early Outfit (Scenes 2-5):** Expensive maroon silk saree with gold border, heavy gold necklace and bangles, sindoor prominent, air of authority
- **Later Outfit (Scenes 7-8):** Same maroon saree but faded, fewer gold bangles, posture diminished, looking smaller and older
- **Arc:** Authoritarian gatekeeper -> irrelevant -> humbled visitor

### HUSBAND (Supporting)
- **Age:** 30
- **Appearance:** Indian man in white vest and lungi, indifferent expression, scrolling phone
- **Appears:** Scene 5 only
